I'm a software engineer living
in Braga, Portugal.
It's lovely here.
We have churches, more churches
and a distinct lack of not churches.
Feel free to follow me on Twitter, GitHub, LinkedIn or Instagram.

I have a MSc in Distributed Systems and Cryptography from University of Minho.

I currently work at Subvisual where I mostly do IoT and blockchain related work.

I am one of the founders of #include <braga>, a volunteering movement that makes use of the superpowers developers have to make a direct positive impact in the local community. We help charities, non-profits and individuals in need through tech.

I am currently the champion of CoderDojo Braga and have been involved with the Dojo for almost 4 years now.

I have won 3 excellence awards for highest grades in Software Engineering, 1 for highest grades in Cryptography, 1 national award for excellence, grit, and being a role model for my peers and the 2016 Pixels Camp Quiz Show which I'm the most proud of.

I also do a lot of open source and have been a regular speaker in conferences, meetups and other university events.

Feel free to email me or hit me up on Twitter and chat about whatever you want to.
